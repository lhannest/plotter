# Plotter

A Flutter app for organizing a novel into scenes and story structure sections.

## Features
- Set overall word count for the novel.
- Specify words per scene and auto-generate scenes for the entire novel.
- Add, edit, delete and reorder scenes.
- Scenes are automatically classified into structural sections:
  - Setup (0% - 12%) – Readers learn about your characters, their goals, and the stakes.
  - Buildup (12% - 25%) – The final pieces necessary for the main conflict are moved into position, while ramping up the tension.
  - Reaction (25% - 37%) – The protagonist scrambles to understand the obstacles thrown in their way by the antagonist.
  - Realization (37% - 50%) – The protagonist's understanding of the conflict grows and their reactions become more informed.
  - Action (50% - 62%) – With their new understanding, the protagonist makes headway against the antagonist.
  - Renewed push (62% - 75%) – The protagonist renews their attack upon the antagonist, seeming to reach a victory.
  - Recovery (75% - 88%) – The protagonist reels as they question their choices, their commitment to the goal, and their own worthiness and ability.
  - Confrontation (88% - 98%) – The protagonist and antagonist duel to the death, so that they cannot both walk away.
  - Resolution (98% - 100%) – Ease the readers out of the excitement and into the final emotion.
- Drag scenes to rearrange them anywhere in the story.
- Save and load the entire novel plot as a JSON file on the device.

## Running
```
flutter run
```
